Who is Karina?

Karina is an all-rounder member of the girl group aespa under SM Entertainment.

Dancer

During her pre-debut, she made an appearance in Taemin's Want. She also appeared with EXO Kai in he collaboration of the virtual showcase between Hyundai Motor Company and SM Entertainment.

Vocalist

Despite not having a vocal position, Karina is also an excellent singer with a deep and husky vocal tone. She have shown her vocal ability in aespa's ballad re-make of Forever and 'Dreams Come True'

Rapper

Because of her deep vocal tone, Karina assumes the position of lead rapper in the group alongside Giselle, the group's main rapper. She also holds the same position in the project group called GOT (Girl's on Top) the Beat.
